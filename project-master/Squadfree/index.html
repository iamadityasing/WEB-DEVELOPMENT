Music and Movie Recommender Chatbot

Music and movie recommendation system will reduce human efforts by searching the huge media collection containing many songs and movies of various genres. Recommendations to the user will be provided according to their mood. The proposed recommendation system works on films and songs in Hindi and English, and can be extended to other languages.
